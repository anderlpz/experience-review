2008–2013 · U.S. Navy

I learned to find the fault
before it found us.

I started in audio — hence the old handle, thesoundman — and found my way into Navy electronics. SATCOM, RF communication systems, SHF and EHF gear. On shipboard equipment, there was no margin for missed faults. You learned to find the problem before it found you, because the systems couldn’t wait and neither could the people depending on them.

That was where the instinct formed: signal, noise, and the discipline to tell them apart.

RF SATCOM SHF/EHF Fault Isolation Electronics

Completed Navy electronics and strategic communications training alongside operational duties.

2013–2022 · Apple

This work shipped in hundreds of millions
of iPhones and AirPods.

Nine years of RF desense analysis, wireless validation, factory test automation, lab debug, and systems integration. The scale still gets me — the same discipline from a ship’s radio room, now applied to products in hundreds of millions of hands. The job was always the same: make the radio behave before the production line started moving.

That work shipped in iPhone 13, iPhone 14, iPhone 15, and AirPods Pro.

The fundamentals carried further than I expected: signal, noise, and where the two get confused.
RF Desense Wireless Validation Factory Test Wi-Fi C++ Swift Systems Integration

Completed B.S. Engineering (ABET-accredited) while working full time at Apple.
